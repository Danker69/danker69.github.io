Facts about climate change.

  • 99% of coral reefs will be lost if we exceed 2 degrees Celsius of global warming.
  • Three-quarters of the land-based environment and about 66% of the marine environment have been significantly altered by human actions.
  • Around 50% of all carbon emissions are emitted by the richest 10% of the world's population
  • Our extinction rate is currently 1,000 times the natural rate.
